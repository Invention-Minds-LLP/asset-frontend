Head of Department (HOD) Guide

You are the department-level authority responsible for approving work orders, asset transfers, disposals, and indents for your department.

Modules You Can Access

You have access to everything a Supervisor can access, plus approval capabilities and additional modules:

Dashboard
Department-level KPIs: open tickets, pending approvals, PM compliance.
Asset Master (Full)
View Assets, Assignments, Transfer, Department Assets, Revenue Log, Asset Disposal.
Asset Indent
Approve or reject asset indent requests from your department.
Store & Inventory
Approve store transfers within your department.
Maintenance (Full)
Repair Tickets, Work Orders, Preventive Maintenance, Calibration, PM Checklists.
Contracts & Coverage
Warranty, Insurance, Service Contracts, Vendor Performance.
Finance & Analytics > Reports
View department-specific asset and maintenance reports.
Operations (Full)
Gate Pass, Acknowledgement, Physical Audit, Employee Exit, Document Vault, Knowledge Base.

What You Can Do

  • ✓ Approve/Reject Repair Tickets raised by your department members
  • ✓ Approve/Reject Work Orders and issue Work Completion Certificates (WCC)
  • ✓ Approve Asset Indents from your department
  • ✓ Approve Asset Transfers in and out of your department
  • ✓ Approve Store Transfers between locations
  • ✓ Approve Service Visit Charges from external vendors
  • ✓ Send assets for Disposal (initiates committee review)
  • ✓ Complete Physical Audits for your department
  • ✓ Approve Gate Pass requests for asset movement
  • ✓ View department-specific Reports
  • ✓ All Supervisor capabilities (execute work orders, PM, etc.)

What You CANNOT Do

  • Access Financial Dashboard, CFO Dashboard, or Batch Depreciation
  • Access Decision Engine or Cost Analysis
  • Configure SLA Matrix, Escalation Matrix, or Support Matrix
  • Manage users, module access, system config, or email templates
  • Access Master Settings or Inventory Master (admin-only)
  • Final-approve disposals (committee/CEO level)

Step-by-Step Workflows

Workflow 1: Work Order Approval and WCC Issuance

  1. Navigate to Maintenance > Work Orders in the sidebar.
  2. Filter by status Pending Approval.
  3. Review the work order details: asset, issue description, estimated cost, and assigned technician.
  4. Click Approve to authorize the work, or Reject with remarks.
  5. Once the supervisor completes the work order, it returns to you for WCC issuance.
  6. Open the completed work order and review the work log and materials consumed.
  7. Click Issue WCC (Work Completion Certificate) to formally close the work order.
  8. The WCC is recorded and linked to the asset's maintenance history.

Workflow 2: Asset Indent Approval

  1. Navigate to Asset Indent in the sidebar.
  2. View pending indent requests from your department.
  3. Review the requested asset type, justification, estimated budget, and urgency.
  4. Click Approve to forward the indent for processing, or Reject with a reason.
  5. Approved indents proceed for further processing.

Workflow 3: Asset Disposal Workflow

  1. Navigate to Asset Master > Asset Disposal in the sidebar.
  2. Click New Disposal Request or find an existing draft.
  3. Select the asset(s) to be disposed of.
  4. Enter the Reason for Disposal (end-of-life, beyond repair, obsolete, etc.).
  5. Submit the request — it is sent to the disposal committee for review.
  6. The committee (Operations/CEO) will make the final decision.
  7. Track disposal status on this same screen.

Workflow 4: How to Use the Department Dashboard

  1. Navigate to Dashboard in the sidebar.
  2. The dashboard displays department-level KPIs by default.
  3. Review key metrics: Open Tickets, Pending Approvals, PM Compliance %, Asset Utilization.
  4. Click on any KPI card to drill down into the detailed list.
  5. Use the date range filter to compare performance across periods.
  6. Check the Pending Approvals widget daily to stay on top of action items.
Tip: Pending approvals are time-sensitive. Delayed WO approvals can trigger SLA breaches and escalations. Check Dashboard > Pending Approvals at the start of each day.
