CEO / COO Guide

You have top-level executive oversight of the entire asset management system. This guide covers strategic dashboards, final approval authority, the Decision Engine, and organization-wide visibility.

Modules You Can Access

Dashboard
Organization-wide overview: all departments, all KPIs.
Asset Master (Full)
View Assets, Assignments, Transfer, Department Assets, Revenue Log, Asset Disposal, Sub-Assets.
Asset Indent
Review high-value indent requests.
Store & Inventory
View inventory levels across all stores.
Maintenance (Full)
Repair Tickets, Work Orders, Preventive Maintenance, Calibration, PM Checklists.
Contracts & Coverage (Full)
Warranty, Insurance, Service Contracts, Vendor Performance.
Finance & Analytics (Full)
Financial Dashboard, CFO Dashboard, Cost Analysis, Decision Engine, Batch Depreciation, Reports.
Operations (Full)
Gate Pass, Acknowledgement, Physical Audit, Employee Exit, Document Vault, Knowledge Base.
Administration (Partial)
SLA Matrix, Escalation Matrix, Notifications, Audit Trail (read access).

What You Can Do

  • ✓ COO Dashboard — organization-wide operational overview
  • ✓ Financial Dashboard — detailed asset valuation, depreciation, and spend
  • ✓ Decision Engine — AI-assisted maintain-vs-replace recommendations
  • ✓ Approve Disposals — final authority on asset disposal decisions
  • ✓ View All Departments — cross-department visibility for all data
  • ✓ Cost Analysis — review TCO and cost-per-usage for strategic decisions
  • ✓ Reports — generate and export all report types
  • ✓ Vendor Performance — review vendor scores for contract renewal decisions
  • ✓ Audit Trail — view system-wide audit trail for compliance

What You CANNOT Do

  • Create or manage user accounts (Admin only)
  • Configure Module Access for roles (Admin only)
  • Edit System Config / Tenant Config (Admin only)
  • Manage Email Templates or SMTP settings (Admin only)
  • Edit Master Settings or Inventory Master data (Admin only)
  • Seed default system data (Admin only)

Step-by-Step Workflows

Workflow 1: How to Review the COO Dashboard

  1. Navigate to Dashboard in the sidebar — the main dashboard provides an organization-wide overview.
  2. Review key operational metrics: Total Open Tickets, SLA Compliance %, PM Compliance %, Asset Utilization Rate.
  3. Use the Department Filter to drill down into specific departments.
  4. Check the Fleet Health section — percentage of assets in Good, Fair, and Critical condition.
  5. Review SLA Breach Trend — are breaches increasing or decreasing over time?
  6. Identify departments with consistently low PM compliance for management attention.
  7. Click any metric card to drill down into the detailed data.

Workflow 2: How to Use the Decision Engine

  1. Navigate to Finance & Analytics > Decision Engine in the sidebar.
  2. The engine analyzes each asset's: age, maintenance cost trend, downtime frequency, book value, and replacement cost.
  3. Assets are categorized as: Maintain (green), Monitor (yellow), or Replace (red).
  4. Click on an asset in the Replace category to see the detailed analysis.
  5. Review the Cost Projection — projected maintenance costs vs. replacement cost over the next 1-3 years.
  6. Use this data to make strategic CapEx decisions for the next budget cycle.
  7. If you agree with a replacement recommendation, initiate the process via Asset Indent or direct the HOD.

Workflow 3: How to Review Uncovered Assets

  1. Navigate to Contracts & Coverage > Warranty in the sidebar.
  2. Filter by Expired status to see all assets with no active warranty coverage.
  3. Navigate to Contracts & Coverage > Insurance and filter by Expired or Not Covered.
  4. Cross-reference with Cost Analysis to identify high-value assets that are unprotected.
  5. Review the CFO Dashboard > Upcoming Renewals section for proactive planning.
  6. Direct the Finance team or relevant HOD to initiate warranty extensions, AMC renewals, or insurance coverage.
  7. Track coverage improvement over time using the Reports module.
Tip: Start your day with the Dashboard for operational health, then check CFO Dashboard for financial health. Review the Decision Engine monthly to stay ahead of asset replacement needs.
