Executive (End User) Guide

You are the person who uses the asset on a daily basis. This guide covers how to view your assets, raise repair tickets, log usage, and acknowledge assignments.

Modules You Can Access

Dashboard
Overview of your assigned assets, open tickets, and notifications.
My Assets
View all assets currently assigned to you with details and history.
Maintenance > Repair Tickets
View your tickets and raise new ones when equipment needs repair.
Maintenance > New Ticket
Create a new repair/service ticket for a faulty asset.
Asset Master > Revenue Log
Log daily usage data (meter readings, revenue generated).
Operations > Acknowledgement
Acknowledge asset assignments and transfers directed to you.
Administration > Notifications
View system notifications relevant to your assets and tickets.

What You Can Do

  • ✓ View all assets assigned to you via My Assets
  • ✓ Raise a new repair ticket via Maintenance > New Ticket
  • ✓ Track the status of your open tickets via Repair Tickets
  • ✓ Log daily usage/revenue data via Revenue Log
  • ✓ Acknowledge asset assignments via Acknowledgement
  • ✓ View warranty and service contract information (read-only)
  • ✓ View your notifications

What You CANNOT Do

  • Approve or reject any tickets, work orders, or transfers
  • Create or edit assets in Asset Master
  • Access Store & Inventory management
  • Access Work Orders, Preventive Maintenance, or Calibration
  • Access Financial Dashboard, CFO Dashboard, Cost Analysis, or Batch Depreciation
  • Access Administration modules (SLA Matrix, Module Access, System Config, etc.)
  • Create or manage users, email templates, or master settings
  • Initiate asset transfers or disposals

Step-by-Step Workflows

Workflow 1: How to Raise a Repair Ticket

  1. Navigate to Maintenance > New Ticket in the sidebar.
  2. Select the asset that needs repair from the Asset dropdown (only your assigned assets appear).
  3. Choose the Issue Category (e.g., Electrical, Mechanical, Software).
  4. Enter a clear Description of the problem.
  5. Set the Priority level (Low, Medium, High, Critical).
  6. Optionally attach photos of the issue using the Attachments section.
  7. Click Submit Ticket. You will receive a ticket number for tracking.
  8. Track progress under Maintenance > Repair Tickets.

Workflow 2: How to Log Daily Usage

  1. Navigate to Asset Master > Revenue Log in the sidebar.
  2. Select the date for which you are logging data.
  3. Find your asset in the list or search by asset code.
  4. Enter the Meter Reading or Usage Hours for the day.
  5. Enter Revenue Generated if applicable to your asset type.
  6. Add any Remarks (e.g., downtime reasons).
  7. Click Save to submit the log entry.

Workflow 3: How to Acknowledge an Asset Assignment

  1. You will receive a Notification when an asset is assigned or transferred to you.
  2. Navigate to Operations > Acknowledgement in the sidebar.
  3. Find the pending acknowledgement in the list.
  4. Review the asset details — name, code, condition, and location.
  5. Click Acknowledge to confirm you have received the asset.
  6. The asset will now appear under My Assets.
Tip: Always check the Dashboard first when you log in. It shows your pending acknowledgements, open tickets, and any alerts for your assets.
