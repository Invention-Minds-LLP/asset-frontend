Supervisor Guide

You manage your team's assets and maintenance activities. This guide covers work orders, preventive maintenance, and daily oversight tasks.

Modules You Can Access

You have access to everything an Executive can access, plus the following additional modules:

Dashboard
Team-level overview of assets, tickets, and maintenance status.
My Assets
View your own assigned assets.
Asset Master > View Assets
View all assets within your department scope.
Asset Master > Revenue Log
Log and verify daily usage entries from your team.
Asset Master > Department Assets
View assets grouped by department.
Store & Inventory
Issue materials from store for work orders and maintenance.
Maintenance > Repair Tickets
View and assign repair tickets within your scope.
Maintenance > Work Orders
Start, execute, and complete work orders.
Maintenance > Preventive Maintenance
Execute scheduled PM tasks for assets in your area.
Maintenance > PM Checklists
Fill in and submit PM checklist forms during maintenance.
Maintenance > Calibration
Record calibration results for applicable assets.
Operations > Acknowledgement
Acknowledge asset receipts.
Operations > Physical Audit
Verify assets during physical audits.
Contracts & Coverage
View Warranty, Insurance, Service Contracts, and Vendor Performance.
Operations > Knowledge Base
Access maintenance guides and reference documents.

What You Can Do

  • ✓ Start and complete Work Orders assigned to your team
  • ✓ Issue material from Store & Inventory against a work order
  • ✓ Execute Preventive Maintenance tasks and fill PM Checklists
  • ✓ Verify items during Physical Audit
  • ✓ Verify and review Revenue Log entries from team members
  • ✓ Assign and reassign Repair Tickets within your scope
  • ✓ Record Calibration readings
  • ✓ Raise new tickets, log usage, and acknowledge assignments (same as Executive)

What You CANNOT Do

  • Approve or reject Work Orders (you execute, HOD approves)
  • Approve asset disposals or transfers
  • Create new assets or import asset data
  • Access Financial Dashboard, CFO Dashboard, Cost Analysis, or Decision Engine
  • Access Batch Depreciation or Reports
  • Configure SLA Matrix, Escalation Matrix, or Support Matrix
  • Access Module Access, System Config, Email Templates, or Master Settings
  • Create or manage users

Step-by-Step Workflows

Workflow 1: How to Start and Complete a Work Order

  1. Navigate to Maintenance > Work Orders in the sidebar.
  2. Find the work order assigned to you (status: Approved).
  3. Click on the work order to open the detail view.
  4. Click Start Work to change the status to In Progress.
  5. Record the work performed in the Work Log section.
  6. If spare parts were used, click Issue Material to log store items consumed.
  7. Once finished, click Complete Work Order.
  8. Enter the Completion Summary and any follow-up notes.
  9. The work order moves to Completed status for HOD review.

Workflow 2: How to Issue Material from Store

  1. Navigate to Store & Inventory in the sidebar.
  2. Click Issue Material.
  3. Select the Work Order or Ticket this issuance is for.
  4. Search for the required item in the inventory list.
  5. Enter the Quantity to issue.
  6. Confirm the issue — stock levels will update automatically.
  7. The issuance is recorded against the work order for cost tracking.

Workflow 3: How to Execute Preventive Maintenance

  1. Navigate to Maintenance > Preventive Maintenance in the sidebar.
  2. View the PM schedule — find tasks due today or overdue.
  3. Click on a PM task to open it.
  4. Click Start PM to begin execution.
  5. Follow the associated checklist (if linked) by navigating to the PM Checklist.
  6. Record readings, observations, and any parts replaced.
  7. Click Complete PM once all steps are finished.

Workflow 4: How to Fill a PM Checklist

  1. Navigate to Maintenance > PM Checklists in the sidebar.
  2. Find the checklist linked to your current PM task.
  3. Open the checklist — it will display a list of check items.
  4. For each item, mark Pass, Fail, or N/A.
  5. Enter Reading Values where numeric input is required.
  6. Add Remarks for any item that failed or needs follow-up.
  7. Click Submit Checklist to save and link it to the PM record.
Tip: Check Preventive Maintenance daily for overdue tasks. PM compliance is tracked and reported to management. Completing PMs on time helps maintain the department's compliance score.
